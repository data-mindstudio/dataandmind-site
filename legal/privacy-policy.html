Data & Mind Studio (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates websites, mobile applications, and digital products under the domain https://dataandmind.studio.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled across our websites, blogs, mobile applications, and products.

1. Information We Collect

a) Mobile Applications

Our mobile applications are designed to be privacy-first.

  • We do not require user accounts

  • We do not collect personal data

  • All user data (if any) is stored locally on the device

  • We do not store data on our servers

If ads are enabled in an app:

  • Third-party ad providers (such as Google AdMob) may collect anonymous, non-personal data for ad delivery and analytics, governed by their own privacy policies.

b) Websites & Blogs

When visiting our website:

  • No personal data is collected by default

  • Basic, anonymized analytics may be used to understand traffic and improve content

  • No tracking cookies are used unless explicitly stated

If you voluntarily contact us (e.g., email):

  • We only use the information to respond

  • We do not share it with third parties

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and improve our apps, websites, and content

  • To comply with legal obligations

  • To respond to user inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

Our products may use third-party services that collect information independently, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Services

  • Google AdMob (if ads are enabled)

Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We follow reasonable practices to protect information. Since most app data is stored locally on your device, you remain in control of your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our products are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
